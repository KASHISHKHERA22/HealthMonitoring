The pulse rate, measured in beats per minute (BPM), indicates the heart's rhythmic contraction and relaxation. For adults, the average resting pulse rate ranges from 60 to 100 BPM. Factors such as age, fitness level, and emotional state can influence pulse rate. Regular monitoring helps assess cardiovascular health.

Your Temp Is: 90

The human body temperature, typically around 36.5 to 37.5 degrees Celsius (97.7 to 99.5 degrees Fahrenheit), is regulated by the hypothalamus. Variations can signal health conditions. Fever, often indicating infection, raises body temperature, while hypothermia, from exposure or illness, lowers it. Temperature stability is crucial for bodily functions.
